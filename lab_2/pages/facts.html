Fun Facts About Sea Otters

  • Sea otters are the only otters that give birth in the water.
  • They have the densest fur in the animal kingdom, with up to 1 million hairs per square inch.
  • Sea otters hold hands while sleeping to prevent drifting apart.
  • They use rocks to crack open shellfish, making them one of the few tool-using animals.
  • Sea otters are known to have a pouch in their armpit where they store their favorite rocks and food.
  • They can eat up to 25% of their body weight in food each day.
  • Sea otters are considered a keystone species because their presence in an ecosystem affects the whole food web.
  • They have a fur that is not only waterproof but also provides insulation against cold water.
  • Sea otters are very social animals and often float together in groups called rafts.
  • They have pockets in their skin under their forearms for storing food and rocks.
